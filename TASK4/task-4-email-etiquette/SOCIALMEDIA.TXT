SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS
VIT Bhopal University | CSE0001 Digital Literacy

=====================================================
✅ DO'S
=====================================================

1. Keep your profile professional — future employers 
   and colleges will check your social media.

2. Think before you post — once something is online, 
   it is very difficult to remove permanently.

3. Use privacy settings to control who can see 
   your posts and personal information.

4. Engage respectfully in online discussions and 
   debates — disagree politely, never personally.

5. Share your academic achievements, projects, and 
   certifications to build your professional image.

=====================================================
❌ DON'TS
=====================================================

1. Don't share your phone number, home address, or 
   college ID publicly on any platform.

2. Don't post offensive, hateful, or inappropriate 
   content — it can affect your career permanently.

3. Don't accept friend or follow requests from 
   complete strangers.

4. Don't share exam papers, internal college 
   documents, or any confidential information.

5. Don't use social media to bully, troll, harass, 
   or spread rumours about anyone.

=====================================================
📌 REMEMBER
=====================================================
Your digital footprint is permanent. 
Always ask yourself — "Would I be okay if my 
professor or future employer saw this?" before posting.
